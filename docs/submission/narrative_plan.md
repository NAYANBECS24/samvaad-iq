Objective: demonstrate that SAMVAAD-IQ solves Challenge 1 through a complete evidence-grounded workflow, explainable intelligence, honest Catalyst integration, measurable quality, and human-controlled safety.

6. KAVACH exposes weights, contributions, thresholds, and contradictions.
7. Evidence Lab validates bytes, hashes provenance, and separates facts from interpretation.

9. Reproducible measured evaluation and performance.
10. Runtime-verified Catalyst capability matrix and blue-green rollout.
11. Three-minute judge journey and closing position.
